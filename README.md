# HOW TO USE!!

# 1. Blue Boxes = Momentum / RSI Trigger Zones
Yung mga blue blocks sa taas ng candles:
ibig sabihin may strong RSI condition na nangyari
parang “institution attention zone”
kadalasan lumalabas bago:
reversal
continuation
exhaustion
Napansin ko:
noong uptrend (May–Oct) → lumalabas sila habang malakas trend
noong downtrend (Nov–Feb) → halos wala or delayed
ngayon (Apr–May) → bumabalik ulit sila
Meaning:
sinusubukan ng market bumuo ng bagong bullish structure.

# 2. Background Colors (Red/Green)
Ito pinaka-interesting part.
Green Zones
Mukhang:
bullish RSI environment
accumulation phase
momentum recovery
Red Zones
Mukhang:
bearish pressure
distribution
fear environment

# 3. EMA Flow
May dalawang blue lines:
fast EMA
slow EMA
Kapag:
fast nasa ibabaw = bullish flow
fast nasa ilalim = bearish flow
Ngayon sa current area:
medyo nagco-converge
parang nagdadalawang isip market
Kaya tama yung nasa kanan:
Trend: NEUTRAL
Signal: WAIT
Actually mature signal yan.
Hindi pilit bullish.
Hindi pilit bearish.
Professional systems madalas:
“wait first before attack.”

# 4. Daily RSI + 4H RSI Parehas 48
Ito sobrang important.
48 RSI is middle zone.
Meaning:
walang dominance buyers
walang dominance sellers
Parang:
compressed spring.
Kapag ganito:
madalas susunod explosive move
pero kailangan muna ng confirmation

# BUY Conditions
Kapag:
1D RSI > 40
4H RSI > 45
EMA20 > EMA50
price nag retest sa EMA
may bullish candle
THEN:
→ BUY

# SELL / EXIT
Kapag:
RSI above 70
may rejection wick
price sobrang layo sa EMA20
THEN:
→ partial TP

# WAIT Zone
Ito importante.
Kapag:
mixed RSI
sideways
walang trend alignment
Signal:
→ WAIT
Yan yung pumapatay sa emotional trading.
